Workplace Safety Policy Guidelines
1. Ensure all emergency exits are completely clear at all times.
2. Fire drill drills are scheduled for the second Tuesday of each quarter.
3. Locate the nearest fire extinguisher on your floor upon joining.
4. Immediately report any hazard, spill, or injury to safety@company.com.
5. The main first-aid kit is located next to the cafeteria counter.
6. Basic safety training is mandatory for all office coordinators.
7. Do not touch or modify exposed electrical wiring or server panels.
8. Emergency helpline contact number: +1-800-555-0199.
