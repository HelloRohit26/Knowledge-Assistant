Remote work is available for eligible full-time roles.
Core working hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM EST.
Employees must have a secure and stable internet connection.
A monthly home office stipend is provided for internet costs.
Company laptops must be used for all official tasks.
Communication channels like Slack must be active during work hours.
Virtual meetings require cameras to be turned on when feasible.
Performance is evaluated based on output rather than hours logged.
Relocation to another state requires HR and tax approval.
Hybrid employees are expected in the office 2 days a week.
