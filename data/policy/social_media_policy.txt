Employees are encouraged to share company achievements online.
Always clarify that opinions expressed are personal and not corporate.
Do not disclose confidential product roadmaps or financials.
Keep online discussions professional and respectful.
Do not comment on legal matters or competitor disputes.
Company logos should not be used in personal avatars.
Respect copyright and fair use guidelines when sharing media.
Report any social media crises to the PR team immediately.
Avoid engaging with online trolls or hostile comments.
Social media activity during work hours should be kept minimal.
