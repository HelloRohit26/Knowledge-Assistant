We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users.
Personal data is collected only for necessary account operations.
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256.
We comply fully with GDPR and CCPA regulations.
Users have the right to request deletion of their data.
Data is never sold or shared with third-party advertisers.
Cookies are used solely to improve user experience.
Security audits are conducted bi-annually by external firms.
Data breaches will be reported to authorities within 72 hours.
Privacy policy updates will be communicated via email.
