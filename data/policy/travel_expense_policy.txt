Business travel must be approved by the department head.
Flights under 6 hours must be booked in economy class.
Hotel bookings are capped at 150 USD per night standard rate.
Daily meal allowance is set at a maximum of 60 USD.
Rideshares and public transit expenses are fully reimbursable.
Itemized receipts must be provided for all transactions.
Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of travel.
Personal expenses during business trips are not covered.
Company credit cards should be used where possible.
Reimbursement payments are processed with the bi-weekly payroll.
