Commission is calculated as 8% of the contract value.
Accelerators kick in once 100% of quarterly quota is met.
Commission is paid out on the 15th of the following month.
Deals must have a signed contract and first payment received.
Split commissions must be agreed upon before contract signing.
Clawback policy applies if a client cancels within 90 days.
Non-standard discounts reduce the commission rate proportionally.
Quarterly commission statements will be provided for review.
Discrepancies must be reported to Sales Ops within 5 days.
The policy is subject to change at the start of each fiscal year.
