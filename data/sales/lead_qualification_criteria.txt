All incoming leads must be qualified using the BANT framework.
Budget: Confirm the prospect has funding for software solutions.
Authority: Identify the decision-maker and key influencers.
Need: Determine if the product solves a critical business pain point.
Timeline: Establish a purchase decision schedule within 6 months.
Ideal customer profile includes tech companies with 50-500 employees.
Leads must exhibit active engagement with marketing content.
Inbound queries must be answered by an SDR within 2 hours.
Disqualified leads should be nurtured via email campaigns.
Qualified leads are handed over to Account Executives.
