Employees are entitled to 45 paid leaves annually.
Maternity leave is 180 days.
Paternity leave is 15 days.
Sick leave balance is capped at 10 days per year.
Unused paid leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 5 days.
All leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.
Emergency leaves require manager approval within 24 hours of absence.
Casual leave cannot be clubbed with privilege leave without prior permission.
Bereavement leave of up to 5 days is provided for immediate family.
Unapproved absences will lead to loss of pay and potential disciplinary action.
