Annual performance reviews occur during the month of December.
Self-appraisals must be completed by November 30th.
Managers must schedule one-on-one meetings with all direct reports.
Evaluations are based on key performance indicators defined in Q1.
Peer reviews are anonymous and collected via the HR portal.
Ratings range from Unsatisfactory to Exceeds Expectations.
Performance improvement plans are initiated for low ratings.
Compensation adjustments and promotions are linked to these reviews.
All final review documents must be signed by employee and manager.
Signed reviews are archived in the employee's permanent record.
