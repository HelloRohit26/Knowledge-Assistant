All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior.
Harassment or discrimination of any kind is strictly prohibited.
Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to HR immediately.
Respect and collaboration should guide all team interactions.
Proper office attire or video call etiquette is required.
Company resources must be used responsibly and ethically.
Confidential business information must not be shared externally.
Alcohol and illegal substances are not permitted on premises.
Violations of this code will result in disciplinary action.
Serious or repeated offenses may lead to termination of employment.
